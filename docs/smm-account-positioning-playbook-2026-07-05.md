# SMM Playbook — Account Positioning (账号定位手册)

> Drafted 2026-07-05 from `raw/seo-drop-07-05/smm-discussion.txt`. Companion to the
> SMM leg of `workstream-seo-smm-growth.md`. **This supersedes "content strategy" as
> the operating frame for social.**

## Core thesis — 从"发什么"切换到"账号是谁"

The recommendation-feed platforms (X / 小红书 / TikTok / YouTube / FB) do not
reward *good content* in the abstract — they reward **content that matches the
identity the algorithm has already assigned to the account**. The old question
was *"Curify 应该发什么内容？"*. The right question is:

> **每个账号在平台眼里是谁？** (What does the platform think this account *is*?)

The evidence is our own: Jay's X account sat at ~400 impressions as a
tech/data-science identity, then started posting AI-generated images → the algo
relabeled it from **Tech → AI Art**, the audience mismatched, and the account
died. That is **Position Drift**, and it is the single most expensive mistake in
social. Consistency of identity beats per-post polish.

## Evidence from actual top content (2026-07-06)

Analyzed real top-performer screenshots for three accounts (7+5 FB Curify, 5 RedNote
Curify, 10 RedNote Jay). **The data confirms the *framework* (Account Positioning) but
overturns several *content bets* in the memo.** Operational truth = the cheat sheet below.

### FB (Curify) — proven lane: East-Asian language & culture **edutainment**
- **Winning format = the "Sinosphere 3-way comparison infographic"** (Japan vs Korea vs
  China, side-by-side): weddings, education values, greetings, numbers, regional faces.
  Same template drove the top posts (73–114 shares). **This is the single highest-leverage
  FB format we own.** Axes still open: food, festivals, New Year, etiquette, funerals.
- Also proven: language/number **memes** (Friends-reaction, 86 shares), **"Did you know?"
  awe-geography** (Jindo sea-parting, photoreal+overlay), and **MBTI self-ID memes**
  ("which are you?" → `/topics/mbti` — ties FB straight to our Tier-1 SEO cluster).
- **Mechanics:** single image or single video (**never carousels**), **English**, seeded
  into **topical Groups** (SINOSPHERE, "Visiting Korea", MBTI Meme Therapy…), **share-driven**
  (shares > comments), soft CTA to `/topics/*` or a `/nano-template/.../example/...` deep link.
- **Overturns the memo:** FB does **NOT** want founder/"build-in-public" stories — zero winners
  are personal narrative. Carousels confirmed dead. FB's real appetite = *useful / funny / awe*
  culture edutainment, an axis the memo didn't name.

### RedNote (Curify brand) — proven lane: Chinese history/culture **reverse-trivia, text-first**
- **Winners are TEXT essays / quote-cards**, not visual learning objects: 长平之战 what-if
  (10.2K views, **510 comments**), 彝族vs羌族 ethnography, 打更人 etymology. **Video** wins raw
  reach (tornado-escape 12.6K, Jensen-Huang-night-market 3.4K).
- **The intended flagships underperform:** bilingual flashcards, MBTI cards, WC posters all sit
  near the bottom. The "反常识轻科普" half of the hypothesis holds; the "**视觉学习物件**" half is
  **refuted** for organic reach. *(Flashcards remain a great SEO/product asset — just not the
  RedNote hook.)*
- Counterfactual "what-if" framing is a **comment/reach magnet, not a save magnet** — pick it
  when the KPI is algorithmic push, not bookmarks.

### RedNote (Jay personal) — proven lane: **宏观思考 (macro-thinking)** — hypothesis CONFIRMED
- The 100× breakout is repeatable: **"统计学已经不存在了"** = **199.7K views / 3,636 saves /
  1,380 shares.** Clone template = a bold **反差 declaration about the death/rebirth of a whole
  field** ("X 已经不存在了" — disciplines, professions, business models, mental models).
- **Format:** long-form **text-cover essay carousel**, serif/bold declarative headline on white.
- **Saves = the audience bookmarks *frameworks & theses*, not entertainment.** Drift risks
  (proven underperformers): product-promo with visible **#Curify branding suppresses** the
  thought-leader signal even on-brand; **personal classifieds + travel/lifestyle video flatline**.

### Cross-cutting: two engines, and the product-output paradox
1. **The visual learning object (flashcards) — Curify's core product output — is NOT what wins
   organic social reach** on either RedNote or FB. Reach comes from *ideas* (macro-theses),
   *culture-trivia* (text or 3-way infographic), and *awe*. Plan social around those; treat the
   product asset as the CTA payoff, not the hook.
2. **Two distinct engines — don't conflate their KPIs:**
   - **Reach / authority engine** — macro-thinking theses (Jay) + culture reverse-trivia (Curify).
     KPI = shares + algorithmic push (→ 200K). Builds brand.
   - **Utility-virality engine** — free-tool posts (e.g. the subtitle-tool note: 17% save rate,
     ~8K-capped reach). KPI = saves from tool-hunters. Builds a save-count, **not** authority.
   Post both, but measure each on its own KPI and never let utility-promo dominate an authority account.
3. **FB↔SEO synergy:** the FB "Sinosphere comparison" + "MBTI self-ID" lanes map directly onto
   productized templates and the MBTI SEO cluster — one asset, two channels. Prefer FB topics that
   have a `/nano-template` or `/topics` destination so the share funnels into an indexable page.

## Series over one-offs — 运营"栏目"，不是运营"账号"

Hypothesis worth testing: we currently run *accounts* but not *series (固定栏目)*.
One tech post, one startup post, one AI image → the platform can't learn a stable
pattern. Fixed weekly columns give the recommender a legible audience to match.
This **independently validates** the existing themed-day rotation (`93fdf60`,
Mon-MBTI→Sun) — extend that pattern into named series per the table above.

## Platform-specific breakage & the fix

- **TikTok / YouTube — 300-400 播放瓶颈:** 这是典型的算法"基础测试池"——内容被识别了，
  但前 3 秒完播率/互动不足以进入下一级流量池。**先别急着优化视频。** 先做统计:过去 ~100 条里
  哪些破 1000 / 3000 / 10000,共同点是什么。TikTok 反馈比 Google 快得多——用数据找 Hook 模式,
  再改自动化脚本里的前 3 秒。
- **Facebook — Carousel 没起来是正常的:** FB 算法极度偏好个人生活向贴文、社团互动、原生短视频;
  外链导流和纯产品展示会被严重限流。**暂停纯模板 Carousel 分发**,改为在设计/AI 社团里以价值形式分享。

## Operational hook — 自动分发矩阵要遵守 allow/ban

The autopost pipeline (`autopost_utils.py`, currently Twitter + FB, hash-bucketed
cron) must enforce the per-account allow/ban lists above, not blanket-broadcast:
- **Curify X** — fine for hot-topic visual; keep.
- **Curify FB carousel broadcast** — the memo says this is *actively harmful*
  (Position Drift + algo sandbox). **Candidate to pause** pending the group-aware
  routing item already queued in the workstream (SMM "Queued").
- **Jay's accounts** are hand-operated (not in autopost) — the table is a human
  editorial contract for Jay + 红杰 + any agent, so everyone knows 什么能发 / 什么不能发.

## The one highest-ROI action — 先完成《账号定位表》

Not a growth strategy — an **audit**. For each account answer 5 questions from
history (this table is the v1 answer; refine with real data):
1. 平台认为这个账号是谁？（基于历史数据）
2. 哪些内容历史上真正起量？
3. 哪些内容发出去明显拖累账号？（Position Drift 证据）
4. 未来 80% 的内容类型是什么？
5. 未来 20% 用来探索、验证新定位。

ROI is high because it **adds no work** — it just makes every existing post more
consistent, which on recommender-feed platforms matters more than any single
post's polish.
